POORNIMA UNIVERSITY

POORNIMA UNIVERSITY is an private university located in SITAPURA INDUSTRIAL AREA ,JAIPUR, RAJASTHAN, India. The institute was established in 2012 and it is approved by UGC.

Campus

The college has approximately 5000 students, 1000 faculty members, 120 laboratories, one central library, and 150 administrative and supportive staff. The campus is spread over an area of 32 acres and consists of four major buildings, a cafeteria, a coffee store, a fruit juice stand, a breakfast center, NPB, and a fully air-conditioned auditorium with ceiling fans.
